What are mechanical keyboards?

Mechanical keyboards are a type of computer keyboard that offers a unique and tactile typing experience. Unlike traditional rubber dome keyboards, mechanical keyboards use individual mechanical switches for each key. These switches are characterized by their distinct feedback and audible click sound when pressed. This design results in a more precise and satisfying typing feel, making mechanical keyboards popular among gamers, typists, and programmers. Additionally, mechanical keyboards are known for their durability, as the switches are designed to last longer than the rubber domes found in typical keyboards.
